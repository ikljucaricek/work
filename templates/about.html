About

Why Tyg-Ayo?

Tygayo application helps you to get the (home) job done. It does not matter weather it is exchanging of a bulb or a water pipes, or weather you need help or you are the one who would like to offer the service. Tygayo makes the agreement easy and transparent.

Who we are?

We are social enthusiast who want to solve people's problems using skills we have - programming. Simply, we want to make our world better place by making communication between people easier. As communication is a key aspect of every society we believe that providing you with tools for better communication we would make your life easier and less stresful.
